All About Chess


Project Summary

For our team project, we will be building an education portal about chess. This portal will consist of four pages that describe the historcial, transitional, current, and future perspectives of chess. It will also include a home page, an introduction to the team page, a quiz page on the mechanics of the game, and a registration page for users to access the site.
